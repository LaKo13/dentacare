ABOUT DENTACARE

The DentaCare dental clinic has been in business for 15 years. Altogether, there are five highly qualified doctors together with a professional team dedicated to your well-being. Alongside all fields of modern dentistry, we increasingly apply maxillofacial surgery and wrinkle injections.
The clinic’s treatment rooms are located on two floors and house the very latest equipment. In the “Plaza Bendinat” building you can find more doctors, shopping opportunities, several restaurants and an underground car park, which is free of charge for all patients. Coming from Palma it is only a 5 minute drive to the clinic. From Andratx you can reach the clinic within about 20 minutes.

Careful root canal treatment (endodontic therapy) is the only way to preserve a heavily inflamed tooth. During root canal treatment we eliminate the nerve of a tooth. The tooth itself is preserved.

In order to achieve the desired success of root canal treatment, we carry it out with the utmost care and state-of-the-art technology. For this purpose we use ozone or laser sterilisation. By means of these sterilisation methods we achieve absolutely germ-free canals.

Today cranio-mandibular dysfunction (CMD) is amongst the most frequent clinical symptoms in adults. This syndrome can have a considerable negative influence on the well-being of a person.
Stress and misalignment of teeth are, amongst other things, causes of functional disorders. However, problems that are not of dental origin can also be the trigger, e.g. problems of a physical or psychological nature.
Extensive diagnostics help to identify possible causes and to select the appropriate therapy. According to diagnosis, this can range from simple relaxation exercises through a relaxation splint up to the calling in a specialist from other fields such as orthopaedics or physiotherapy. A missing tooth can quickly affect the stability of the neighbouring teeth. The consequence is a shifting of your row of teeth. Also the jaw joint may no longer be completely supported. An implant can help to preserve the positioning of a row of teeth. The implant, which is anchored in the bone, serves as an artificial root for the new tooth. As soon as the implant has healed into the bone, a so-called superstructure (application of crowns and bridges) can be implemented.
Implants are made of pure titanium. This material is completely neutral in the body and is also used in endoprosthetics (e.g. for hip joints). In our clinic we use different implant systems from leading manufacturers. We are equipped to solve each individual situation with the best suitable implant. It is very important for us to be able to rely on a quality that fulfils the appropriate requirements.

Misalignment of teeth can impair their stability and aesthetic appearance.
Through the application of innovative materials and optimised treatment techniques, which also offer an aesthetic solution, orthodontic treatment can be carried out quickly whilst avoiding tissue damage.
The decision as to whether the patient receives a fixed or removable brace depends on the scale of dental misalignments and dislocation of jaw as well as the patient’s age, and must be resolved individually in the course of an examination.
In our clinic all children receive as much attentive care and time as they need. Dentistry for children and young adults is our contribution to the health of the younger generation, with the aim of helping to establish a trusting relationship and avoiding anxiety.
We try to make sure that intensive prevention, mostly in the form of an individual prophylaxis, comes before any other restorative therapies are done. The health of your teeth is based on three foundations: nutrition, oral hygiene and fluoride.
Prevention not only includes the sealing of fissures and enamel hardening, but also an examination at least every 6 months. During this procedure we stain deposits and provide an oral hygiene index, which enables parents to monitor the cleaning success of their loved ones.
Periodontal diseases, diseases of the tooth support apparatus are, next to caries, one of the most frequent diseases of our masticatory system.
Thus, 95% of persons over 35 years of age suffer from a more or less serious disease of the periodontium, often without even knowing about it. Even serious periodontal diseases can be successfully treated through an antibacterial treatment of the whole oral cavity (full mouth therapy) and a specific antibiotic therapy, once the germ has been identified.
Our practice is specialised in the prevention and treatment of periodontal diseases, with the aim of providing you with healthy and strong teeth for a lifetime.
The prevention of individual risks is a central consideration for us. To preserve the healthy condition of your teeth throughout your life, special preventive measures are necessary. Extensive prophylaxis measures create the best prerequisites to avoid dental restorations or to keep them at a low level. A safe way to prevent caries and periodontal diseases is achievable through professional tooth cleaning in our practice. In this procedure, tartar and plaque – the causes of caries and periodontosis – are thoroughly and gently removed.
In addition to domestic oral hygiene, professional tooth cleaning should be carried out up to four times a year depending on the individual periodontosis risk. At Clinica Bendinat, prophylaxis is carried out only by specially qualified personnel.
All employees are certified dental hygienists or have completed additional prophylaxis training. To provide optimum protection from caries and periodontal diseases we offer you the following preventive services: Professional tooth cleaning, Guidance for optimum oral hygiene, Analysis of bacterial plaque, Nutritional advice, Fluoride application and Fissure sealing.
